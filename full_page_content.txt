Product Details

The Supercycle Reaction is a versatile and well-equipped hardtail mountain bike. The integrated grip shifting system allows you to cycle through 18 different speeds in various riding conditions, while the front and rear V-brakes provide ample stopping power. The 19-inch frame is designed to fit a variety of riders.
